Business Insights

1. Online stores contributed the highest share of total sales with nearly 38% contribution.

2. South region generated the maximum sales among all regions, followed by Offline and Franchise channels.

3. Desk and Washing Machine were the top-performing products based on sales value.

4. The company maintained a healthy profit margin of 18.49%, indicating strong profitability.

5. Monthly sales remained stable throughout the year with noticeable growth during later months.

6. Brands like WorkWood and SpinWash generated high sales as well as strong profits.

7. Some products showed high sales volume but comparatively lower profit contribution, indicating possible pricing or cost optimization opportunities.

8. Geographic analysis shows strong sales concentration in major Indian states like Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Tamil Nadu.

9. The dashboard helps identify profitable products, regions, and store types for better business decision-making.

10. Interactive filtering enables dynamic analysis by year and customer segment.
